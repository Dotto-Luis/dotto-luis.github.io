Philosophy

Why GICS?

ML is a tool. What matters is the problem it solves and the sector it operates in — not the algorithm.

GICS makes that explicit. Each project targets a real use case in a specific industry. Domain context isn't decoration; it's what turns a model into a solution.

  • End-to-end by default — pipeline, model, tests, and documentation. Every project.
  • LLM evaluation background — 2 years at Scale AI building intuition for reliable AI outputs.
  • Production mindset — FastAPI, Docker, deployment. Not notebooks.
