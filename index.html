About

Why GICS?

Most ML portfolios are a random collection of Kaggle notebooks. Mine is organized by industry sector — the same classification system used by institutional investors worldwide.

This structure reflects how I think: domain context matters. A credit risk model is fundamentally different from a sentiment classifier, even if both use gradient boosting.

  • End-to-end by default — every project includes data pipeline, model, tests, and documentation.
  • LLM evaluation background — 2 years assessing model quality at Scale AI, building intuition for what makes AI outputs reliable.
  • Production mindset — FastAPI, Docker, deployment, not just notebooks.
